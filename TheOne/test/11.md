TC-011: More Than 2 Primary Channels Triggers Focus Failure
Purpose

Ensure channel focus guardrail works.

Preconditions

Channel selection screen available

Steps

Select 3 or more channels as primary

Try to proceed

Expected Results

Block or force prioritization down to one primary plus one secondary

If allowed with override, it generates: high risk flag and requires justification

Final state contains only primary and secondary channels, not a list
