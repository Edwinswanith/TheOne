TC-003: MCQ Recommended Option Saves Without Justification
Purpose

Confirm recommended MCQ choice does not require justification and is stored correctly.

Preconditions

Intake questions available

Test Data

Question: Primary buyer role

Answer type: mcq

Choice: Head of Sales

Marked as system recommended: yes

Steps

Choose the recommended MCQ option

Continue to next question

Inspect stored intake answer

Expected Results

UI allows progress without justification text

Stored answer has:

answer_type = mcq

value = "Head of Sales"

justification absent or empty

meta.source_type is not forced to evidence, typically inference or assumption

No warning banner shown
